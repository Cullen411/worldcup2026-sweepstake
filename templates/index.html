📜 Rules & How to Play

        


            

                
⚽ What is this?

                
The World Cup 2026 SweepStake is an informal sweepstake for the FIFA World Cup 2026 (USA · Canada · Mexico). Every participant is randomly assigned a number of national teams from the 48-team tournament. Your prize depends entirely on how your teams perform.

            


            

                
🎲 The Draw

                
    
                    
  • Every participant is guaranteed at least one seeded team (FIFA top 30 ranked) — no one gets all minnows.
    • 
                    
  • Teams are assigned randomly from two pots: Pot 1 (top 30 by FIFA ranking) and Pot 2 (the rest).
    • 
                    
  • The number of teams per player depends on how many participants enter. With 16 players and 48 teams, each player gets 3 teams.
    • 
                    
  • The draw is final once run — no swaps or trades.
    • 
                    
  • If a team withdraws from the tournament before it starts, a replacement draw will be made for the affected participant.
    • 
                

            


            

                
💰 Prize Fund & Entry

                
    
                    
  • Entry fee is set by the organiser and displayed in the Prize Fund panel.
    • 
                    
  • All entry fees go into the pot — no rake, no organiser cut.
    • 
                    
  • Prizes are paid out after the World Cup Final on 19 July 2026.
    • 
                

            


            

                
🏆 Prizes

                

                    

                        🥇
                        

                            1st Place — 70% of pot
                            Your team wins the World Cup Final
                        

                    

                    

                        🥈
                        

                            2nd Place — 20% of pot
                            Your team is beaten finalist in the World Cup Final
                        

                    

                    

                        🏆
                        

                            Top Scorer Award — 10% of pot
                            You own the team of the tournament's Golden Boot winner (most goals scored)
                        

                    

                    

                        🏳️
                        

                            Lowest Team Goals Award — no cash
                            Your teams collectively score the fewest goals across the entire tournament. Wooden spoon glory only.
                        

                    

                

            


            

                
❓ Tiebreakers

                
    
                    
  • 1st/2nd place tie: If the same participant owns both finalist teams, they take 1st place prize (70%) and 2nd place (20%) is split equally among all other participants.
    • 
                    
  • Top Scorer tie: If two players share the Golden Boot, the prize goes to whoever owns the team of the player who scored first. If neither team is in the sweepstake, the 10% rolls into the 1st place prize.
    • 
                    
  • Lowest Team Goals tie: Joint wooden spoon shame — both participants share the dishonour.
    • 
                

            


            

                
📋 General

                
    
                    
  • This is a friendly sweepstake for entertainment purposes only.
    • 
                    
  • The organiser's decision is final in any dispute.
    • 
                    
  • Results are taken from the official football-data.org API.
    • 
                    
  • Please gamble responsibly — BeGambleAware.org
    •
